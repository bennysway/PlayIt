The app

PlayIt is a multi-platform music streamer, developed for Android, Apple and Windows devices. It combines the power of a device's built in music function with the media soure of YouTube.

What makes this app different from using Youtube is the ability to focus on playlists, details, and infomation about songs instead of the music video. One of the best feature is when you search. No matter what keywords you use, the results will always be music videos and songs

Inspiration

This app was inspired by Spotify and Youtube. Spotify enables you to save playlists for eternity. Youtube offers a wide range of songs that are sometimes not available on Spotify

Powered by

This app was made with Ionic Cordova. This is to give different devices the same experience, from Android to Apple. It is backed by GitHub. We can say that is the safest online code notebook so far. It is coded in these web languages : HTML (page template), Transcript (scripting), Angular (backend scripting) and CSS (design and colors). It is hosted by Firebase. Firebase keeps the app online and manages the database and cloud computing.
